  • Operation of video management policy
  • Operation of video management policy

  • Paradise City (hereinafter referred to as "the Company") will inform you of the purpose and manner in which video information processed by us is used and managed through the operation and management of video information processing devices.

    1. Installation basis of video information processing equipment and purpose of installation
    The Company installs and operates video information processing devices for the following purposes in accordance with Article 25, Paragraph 1 of the Personal Information Protection Act, Article 28 of the Tourism Promotion Act, and Article 36 of the Enforcement Rule of the Tourism Promotion Act.
    1) Facility safety and fire prevention
    2) Prevention of crime for customer's safety
    3) Prevention of misconduct related to sales and accounting
    4) Prevention of vehicle theft and breakage
    * If the parking lot exceeds 30 cars, it can be installed and operated on the basis of Article 6, Section 11 of the 「Enforcement Rule of the Parking Lot Act」

    2. Installation number, installation position and shooting range

    3. Management officer and access authority
    In order to protect your image information and to handle complaints related to personal image information,

    4. Photographing time, storage period, storage place and method of image information
    - How to process: Records / manages matters related to requests such as non-purpose use, third party provision, destruction, and viewing of personal image information,
    Persistent deletion (shredding or incineration for output) in a way that can not be restored when the storage period expires.

    5. Matters concerning entrustment such as installation and management of image information processing device
    We are entrusted with the installation and management of image information processing equipment as described below. In accordance with the relevant laws and regulations, we provide necessary information so that personal information can be managed safely when contracted.

    6. Matters on how to check personal information and place
    - Confirmation method: Contact the person who has right of access to the video information in advance and check it if you visit us
    - Confirmation place: Our security control room to check personal video information

    7. Measures for requests such as viewing information of the information subject
    You may ask the operator of the video information processing device at any time if you wish to view, confirm or delete your personal image information. However, it is limited to the personal image information you have photographed and the personal image information that is clearly required for the benefit of the life, body and property of the information subject. We will take necessary measures without delay when we ask for confirmation or deletion of personal image information.

    8. Measures to ensure safety of image information
    The image information processed by our company is managed safely through encryption measures. In addition, as a management measure for the protection of personal image information, we grant different access rights to personal information, and in order to prevent the tampering of personal image information, the date and time of creation of personal image information, the purpose of reading at the time of reading, And so on. In addition, locks are installed for safe physical storage of personal image information.

    9. Matters concerning changes in personal information processing policy
    This video information processing device operation and management policy was revised on November 30, 2017. If there is any addition, deletion or modification of contents due to change of laws, policies or security technologies, We will notify you of the reasons for change and contents.
    - Operation and management of image information processing equipment, implementation date: November 30, 2017
